You never want to seem impressed with anything about the item in question or any offer they make. As soon as they see you're impressed, they'll start shoving that aspect down your throat and honestly, it's more annoying and distracting than anything else. But that's mostly the point. They want you to buy fast and be out the door.

If the item is pre-owned or used, you're in a better position. It might seem sleazy but try and look for ANY imperfection. Even the slightest thing can drastically reduce the worth of an item.
