King Cash Strategy

Offer cash. Businesses LOVE cash. Charging costs them money, so by offering cash, literally everyone (except Visa or whatever) wins. If you're going for a deep offer, offer it in cash. It makes it so much more appealing to the business. Also offer to pay in full, if you can.

BREAKDOWN OF “SUCCESSFUL HAGGLES”

  • 40% Car
  • 26% Bank Fees
  • 15% Rent
  • 5% Mortgage
