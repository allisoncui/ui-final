GENUITY

If you're going to haggle, you should try to actually have a valid reason for why.

Is it because other stores have it at a more reasonable price?

Is it purely because you want a deal? (avoid this, this is basically just having no reason)

Is it because you like this store and want to see them get more revenue?

A clerk is more inclined to give you a discount if they genuinely believe they're helping you out, and all stores are not going to offer you a discount at a loss, so you're helping them out as well.
