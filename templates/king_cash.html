KING CASH

Offer cash! Businesses LOVE cash. Paying by card costs them money, so by offering cash, literally everyone (except Visa or whatever) wins.

When you're ready to make a significant offer, especially for large purchases or services, proposing a cash payment can be particularly persuasive. If it's feasible, consider paying the entire amount in cash. This not only simplifies the transaction but also demonstrates your commitment and financial readiness, which can be appealing to sellers and may help in securing a favorable deal.
