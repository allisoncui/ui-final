DO NOT DO!

BE CAREFUL OF WHAT YOU SAY OR SUGGEST.

Any of the previous strategies will not be effective if you do any of the following:

  • Reverse auctioning. If they offer $500, just coming back with saying "$300!" won't get you anywhere.
  • Do not have O reason. You need to be able to back up your offer at least a little bit. Bring up other places offering similar items for a price close to yours.
  • You still want to be friendly and approachable. You're asking the business to do you a favor. Make them WANT to cut you a deal by being friendly and open to negotiation.
