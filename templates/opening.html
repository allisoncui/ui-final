OPENING

THE BEST OPENING METHODS ARE THE SIMPLEST.

Just ask "This is a bit more than I’m willing to pay, would you be willing to come down on the price?" This non-confrontational strategy facilitates open communication, creating a conducive environment for friendly negotiation.
